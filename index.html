About CSE Department



We warmly welcome you to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Golden Valley Integrated Campus(GVIC). JUST started its journey on 2007, with five departments among which the department of CSE was the first department. Since then, CSE has been widely recognized for its excellent teaching capabilities. The department provides an outstanding opportunity for students to get a quality education in Computer Science and Engineering. The objectives of the department are:

To equip graduates,post graduates with essential programming language skills for pursuing careers as skilled computer professionals To train students to analyze complex engineering problems and develop efficient and effective solutions To enable students to carry out collaborative and innovative research across diverse fields of computer science To teach students lifelong learning abilities so that they can successfully adapt to evolving technology throughout their careers. To develop in students the interpersonal, communication, and teamwork abilities necessary for working with people in a professional environment. To train graduates in applying the social and ethical dimensions of contemporary computing technology to enhance societal well-being.

Currently, we offer the following programs

  • Diploma in Computer Engineering
  • B.Tech in CSE
  • B.Tech in CSE(IOT)
  • M.Tech in CSE
